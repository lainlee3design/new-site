Title: T-world #7 Review
----
Author: Lain 3
----
Date: 2012-03-30 17:53:09

Text: If you read <a href="http://lainlee3design.com/eddie-z-of-t-world">my post the other day</a>, you'd know that I have been anxiously awaiting my copy of the latest issue of T-World Journal to come from Australia!!!  Well, within two days of my last post and meeting with <a href="http://lainlee3design.com/eddie-z-of-t-world">Eddie Zammit of T-world</a>, it has arrived!!!  And you and I are going to explore it for the first time, together!  Let's dive in!
<p style="text-align: center;"><a href="http://lainlee3design.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/03/IMG_2515.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-1755 aligncenter" title="IMG_2515" src="http://lainlee3design.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/03/IMG_2515-614x458.jpg" alt="" width="553" height="412" /></a></p>
<p style="text-align: left;">I'm lucky I got this at all.  My numbskull of a mail carrier is too lazy to bring parcels up to our door, so she left it in the communal "junk mail"  basket.  Oooh, if it'd been stolen, I'd be livid!!!</p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><a href="http://lainlee3design.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/03/IMG_2518.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-1756 aligncenter" title="IMG_2518" src="http://lainlee3design.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/03/IMG_2518-614x458.jpg" alt="" width="553" height="412" /></a></p>
<p style="text-align: left;">First impressions pulling the magazine out of its protective cover is, "Wow!"  The texture of the hardback cover, the layout and bold presentation, scream "quality" right out of the package!</p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><a href="http://lainlee3design.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/03/IMG_2519.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-1757 aligncenter" title="IMG_2519" src="http://lainlee3design.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/03/IMG_2519-614x458.jpg" alt="" width="553" height="412" /></a></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><a href="http://lainlee3design.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/03/IMG_2521.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-1759 aligncenter" title="IMG_2521" src="http://lainlee3design.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/03/IMG_2521-614x458.jpg" alt="" width="553" height="412" /></a></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><a href="http://lainlee3design.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/03/IMG_2522.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-1760 aligncenter" title="IMG_2522" src="http://lainlee3design.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/03/IMG_2522-614x458.jpg" alt="" width="553" height="412" /></a></p>
<p style="text-align: left;">The matte paper stock only adds to the quality of the publication.  Along with <a href="https://www.facebook.com/ThisIsNowhereFamous" target="_blank">Nowhere Famous</a>, T-world was really inspired by the vintage, New York-style billboards for the typography and layout of this issue, and it shows.  Really adds a level of sophistication to a t-shirt publication.</p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><a href="http://lainlee3design.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/03/IMG_2523.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-1761 aligncenter" title="IMG_2523" src="http://lainlee3design.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/03/IMG_2523-614x458.jpg" alt="" width="553" height="412" /></a></p>

<p style="text-align: center;"><a href="http://lainlee3design.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/03/IMG_2525.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-1763" title="IMG_2525" src="http://lainlee3design.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/03/IMG_2525-614x458.jpg" alt="" width="553" height="412" /></a></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><a href="http://lainlee3design.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/03/IMG_2527.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-1765" title="IMG_2527" src="http://lainlee3design.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/03/IMG_2527-614x458.jpg" alt="" width="553" height="412" /></a></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><a href="http://lainlee3design.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/03/IMG_2529.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-1767" title="IMG_2529" src="http://lainlee3design.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/03/IMG_2529-614x458.jpg" alt="" width="553" height="412" /></a></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><a href="http://lainlee3design.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/03/IMG_2532.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-1770" title="IMG_2532" src="http://lainlee3design.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/03/IMG_2532-614x458.jpg" alt="" width="553" height="412" /></a></p>
<p style="text-align: left;">T-world features more than just those who wear and design t-shirts.  It also takes the time to highlight other elements of the t-shirt world, such as details from t-shirts like neck labels and hangtags, as well as "Must Reads" you can find at your local bookstore.</p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><a href="http://lainlee3design.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/03/IMG_2534.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-1771" title="IMG_2534" src="http://lainlee3design.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/03/IMG_2534-614x458.jpg" alt="" width="553" height="412" /></a></p>
<p style="text-align: left;">How bad-ass is this?  T-world was able to interview the legendary Milton Glaser for the NY Issue.  For those who don't know, Mr. Glaser is the creator of the iconic "I ♥ NY" t-shirt logo, probably the most recognizable and frequently parodied design in history.  Eddie told me that getting a photo opp with the illustrious Mr. Glaser was next to impossible.  When they arrived at his workshop, he opened the door and literally said, "You've got 5 minutes."  Haha!  With that, Eddie and his photographer stepped in, set up, and captured this awesome shot.</p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><a href="http://lainlee3design.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/03/IMG_2535.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-1772" title="IMG_2535" src="http://lainlee3design.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/03/IMG_2535-614x458.jpg" alt="" width="553" height="412" /></a></p>
<p style="text-align: left;">Eddie's favorite part about Mr. Glaser's studio, is that his desk had no computer.</p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><a href="http://lainlee3design.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/03/IMG_2537.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-1773 alignnone" title="IMG_2537" src="http://lainlee3design.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/03/IMG_2537-614x458.jpg" alt="" width="553" height="412" /></a></p>
On the back inside cover, the iconic T-world logo.  Eddie's background in design and attention to details truly show in his brand mark.  He's making t-shirts sophisticated!
<p style="text-align: center;"><a href="http://lainlee3design.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/03/IMG_2538.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-1774" title="IMG_2538" src="http://lainlee3design.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/03/IMG_2538-614x458.jpg" alt="" width="553" height="412" /></a></p>
<p style="text-align: left;">On the back cover, a great closing quote by Jeff Staple.</p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><a href="http://lainlee3design.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/03/IMG_2539.jpg"><img class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-1775" title="IMG_2539" src="http://lainlee3design.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/03/IMG_2539-614x458.jpg" alt="" width="553" height="412" /></a></p>
<p style="text-align: left;">All-in-all, a DYNAMIC publication put together by Eddie and T-world!  Amazing gallery-style photos, vintage billboard-style typography, intuitive layout and great insights into the world of t-shirts make this a MUST-HAVE for every designer and t-shirt connosieur out there!  The quality, hardbound cover, paper stock and print quality (all curated by Eddie's meticulous eye), make this "magazine" well worth the money!</p>
