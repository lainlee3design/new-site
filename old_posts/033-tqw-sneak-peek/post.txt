Text: Just finished polishing off a few new designs for my Three Qlty Wares t-shirt collection and wanted to give you all a sneak peek into the freshness!  Please be sure to share on FB or Retweet this post!

<strong>1) Both Ends</strong>

As solopreneurs, we're always on our hustle.  We don't do the 9-5, we  work bell to bell and then some, literally burning the candle at both  ends.  Many people have shirts about 24-7/365 and never sleeping, so I wanted to go in a different direction with this one.  Similar idea, different direction.<strong>
</strong>

<strong>2) Thief in the Night</strong>

It's been said, "A little sleep, a little slumber, a little folding of the hands to rest; so shall your poverty come like a prowler."  Complacency and laziness will always be your downfall, so this is a little reminder to neither sleep nor slumber, otherwise you'll be poor in life and spirit before you know it.  Never rest.

<strong>3) Trois (Fr. "Three)</strong>
