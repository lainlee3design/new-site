----
Author: Lain 3
----
Date: 2011-10-25 14:33:45
----
Text: Last week, I hit up a few of the homies from <a href="http://instagr.am/" target="_blank">Instagram</a> (<a href="http://friks84.com" target="_blank">Friks84</a>, <a href="http://epicuno.blogspot.com" target="_blank">Epic1144</a> &amp; Edgarzilla) to see if they'd like to collaborate on an illustrated piece.  I was thrilled when they responded emphatically, pencils at the ready, prepared to get down in the illest way!  I proposed a concept and within 24 hours we were all gettin' busy on our contributions to the piece.

The theme is <strong>"Grown Ass Doodlers"</strong> - the final piece will be revealed this Friday, October 28th.  I can't wait to have the final composition all put together.  I'm especially excited since this is my first collaborative project with other artists from around the country, and I'm hoping this opens the door for us all to continue to work together and even do future collabos with more artists!  Below is a little sneak peek at what we've got so far via Instagram!
