Text: We’ve just dropped two new editions of our “99 Problems” tee!  These tees are perfect for the sneakerhead in your life and are designed to match their new white <a href="http://www.nicekicks.com/2012/05/nike-air-yeezy-2-release-date/" target="_blank">Air Yeezy II Platinums</a>, the latest kicks from Kanyeezy himself.  We also have a special colorway to go with your new <a href="http://www.nicekicks.com/shoes/air-jordan-7/" target="_blank">Olympic 7's</a>, dropping July 21 here in the U.S.  Finally, we have two colorways for the <a href="http://www.nicekicks.com/2011/07/retrospect-nike-air-max-90-infrared/" target="_blank">Air Max 90s</a>, a classic staple in the sneakerhead's arsenal, as well as the new <a href="http://www.nicekicks.com/shoes/air-jordan-4-3/" target="_blank">Red, Black and White Jordan 4s</a> dropping August 4th!  Oh, and as an added bonus, each “99 Problems” shirt sold this summer comes with a download of “Can I Kick It?”, a digital track by the homie <a href="http://www.myspace.com/brandontimothy" target="_blank">Brandon Timothy</a>.  It’s a perfect combination for “kickin’” off your summer right!
