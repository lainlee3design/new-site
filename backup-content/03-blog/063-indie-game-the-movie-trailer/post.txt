Text: James Swirsky and Lisanne Pajot of Winnipeg, Manitoba, Canada have been getting great reviews this year with their film "Indie Game: The Movie", a thrilling look behind the scenes into the world of Indie Game Developers.  An official selection of the 2012 Sundance Film Festival, Indie Game: The Movie captures the emotional journey of these meticulously obsessive artists who devote their lives to their interactive art. Four developers, three games, and one ultimate goal— to express oneself through a video game.
