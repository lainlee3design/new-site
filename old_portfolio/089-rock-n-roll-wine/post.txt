Title: Rock-N-Roll Wine
----
Date: 2012-01-28 01:50:20
----
Text: Rock-N-Roll Wine is a Las Vegas original, a company that pairs the experience of wine tasting with the exhilaration of a rock concert, by pairing the flavor of their wine with different musical artists.  The client requested that we come up with some fun t-shirt ideas that their supporters would love to wear.  The challenge, of course, was designing something that wasn't so rock-&amp;-roll that the wine lovers wouldn't wear it, and something that wasn't so "wine-lover-y" that rock-&amp;-rollas wouldn't wear it.
