Text: Gtown™ is a coupon-based website that allows users to create, share, and promote their own coupons.  The guys at Gtown™ approached LainLee3Design with a three-tiered request: (1) they needed to beef up their branding and update it to match the new visual direction they wanted to take their company in; (2) they needed some character design to complement the branding; and (3) they needed a fresh new website design to top it all off.
