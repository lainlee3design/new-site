Text: T10 is the brainchild of Brandon Middleton, engineer, musician, Renaissance man, genius.  T10 stands for "Tic Tac Toe Ten", his game that fuses the familiar look of traditional tic tac toe and the strategy of chess to create a whole new gaming experience.  You can check T10 out at <a href="http://tictactoeten.com" target="_blank">http://tictactoeten.com</a> or download it in either the Android or iPhone marketplaces.
