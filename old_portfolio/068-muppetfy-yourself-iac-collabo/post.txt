Date: 2011-12-15 02:41:14

Text: In honor of the recent release of "The Muppet Movie", the IAC (Instagram Artist Collective) decided to illustrate Muppet versions of ourselves!  What ensued was another dynamite fusion of 4 completely different styles to comprise a solid finished piece.  You can read more about the piece here, as well as see the other artists we inspired around the web to participate in the "Muppetfy Yourself" project.
