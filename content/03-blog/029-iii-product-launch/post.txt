Text: It is my esteemed pleasure to announce the launch of <em>two of the first products</em> from <strong><span style="color: #2296df;">LainLee3Design</span></strong> in the <a href="http://lainlee3design.bigcartel.com" target="_blank"><strong>III Store</strong></a> - <strong>"Mr. Three Fingers" T-Shirt</strong> and the <strong>"Bronson" Print</strong>.  Each of these is a flagship design to be launched for their respective lines.

"Mr. Three Fingers" is my flagship design for my new line, <span style="color: #2296df;"><strong>Three Qlty Wares</strong></span>.  This line will feature designs that embody the freelance mentality and personality, featuring both original artwork and collaborations with other amazing artists.  As with any young clothing line, I'm still developing and discovering what <span style="color: #2296df;"><strong>Three Qlty Wares</strong></span> really is, but that's all apart of the journey.  I'm currently accepting pre-orders for "Mr. Three Fingers", which will start shipping the first week of May 2011.

"Bronson" is not only the first ever print to be released by <strong><span style="color: #2296df;">LainLee3Design</span></strong> but is also the flagship print of my other line, <span style="color: #2296df;"><strong>III Cards &amp; Prints™</strong></span>, which will be debuting later on this year.  Inspired by the film of the same name, Bronson is one bad lil' dude.  You'll be seeing much more of this little guy in the future.  The "Bronson" Print will be available for sale in the <strong><a href="http://lainlee3design.bigcartel.com/" target="_blank"><strong>III Store</strong></a></strong> on April 25, 2011, so save up those pennies boys &amp; girls!
